Queries

  1. To retrieve the names of all goods that have the letter "i" in their name and the customer's name contains the letter "j".
  2. Find the total number of customers in the Customer table and the details of the customer with ID 102, including their contact number.
  3. Retrieve the name and contact information of all customers who have purchased the goods with the lowest price in the Goods table.
  4. Find the customers who have not placed any orders yet.
  5. Find the name of the customer who placed the most expensive order.
  6. List all customers who have made more than 2 orders.
  7. Drop the three tables.
